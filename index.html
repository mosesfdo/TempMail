About This Project

This is a **temporary email service** designed to provide you with disposable email addresses. Use it for quick sign-ups, testing, or whenever you want to avoid giving out your primary email address.

Key features include:

  • Instant email address generation
  • A temporary inbox for receiving messages
  • Easy copy-to-clipboard functionality for your generated email
  • A simple and intuitive user interface

Your privacy is important. Emails generated and received here are temporary and not stored long-term.
